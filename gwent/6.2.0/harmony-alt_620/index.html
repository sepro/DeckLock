This is one of the Decks that got me to Pro Rank for the first time (Season of the Viper, 2020). While this deck still has a lot of Harmony Engines, it also has Toruviel and Pavko Gale for a bit more control over the board. This was better for the meta up in the higher ranks (7-1).
